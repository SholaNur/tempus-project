About Us

Tempus Facilities Maintenance, your trusted partner for all your home maintenance and repair needs in Washington state. Our company was founded by Alex Vehorn, a seasoned expert in home repair and maintenance, who has been serving residents in the region for many years. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to delivering top-notch services, Alex and his team are dedicated to helping homeowners maintain their properties with ease and convenience.

From electrical repairs and carpentry work to painting and drywall repairs, we offer a comprehensive range of services to meet your unique needs. At Tempus Facilities Maintenance, we take pride in our attention to detail and strive to deliver the highest quality workmanship on every project, big or small. Our goal is to help you enjoy a well-maintained home, while saving you time, money, and hassle.

Services

Selling Your Home

Our interior services include repairing cracks and holes in drywall, replacing screen doors, painting rooms, ensuring that doors work properly, repairing or replacing individual tile backsplashes or floor tiles, replacing old caulking around bathtubs and sinks, installing a new mirror, and installing shelving to help you maximize your space.

Property Maintenance

Our expert team is equipped to handle everything from clearing out debris and trash to power washing surfaces and disinfecting high-touch areas. We'll make sure your property is sparkling clean and ready for its next occupants. We're focused solely on interior and exterior cleaning and maintenance.
